Hot n' Spicy

Welcome to PRAPP, the prank generating app! Do you love playing pranks on people? If so, PRAPP can help you find the perfect prank to play on your family, friends and even people that you don't know! PRAPP is for people that love pulling off the perfect practical joke. For those with a great sense of humor, a great prank is one that can takes some creativity, follow through and great timing! With PRAPP, you can choose the degree of prank that you would like to play upon an unsuspecting victim. If further customization is needed you can select further choices to customize a generated prank.
